St.Daniel University: Empowering Minds, Shaping Futures

St.Daniel University is a beacon of education in South Sudan, committed
to nurturing young minds and fostering holistic development.
Our mission is to provide quality education to conflict-affected youths, empowering them with knowledge, skills, and values that transcend borders. As we look to the future, our vision is to be a center of excellence, promoting innovation, cultural heritage, and community service.

History of the University

Early Roots: Established in the early 1920s, St.Daniel University has a rich legacy. The Church Missionary Society (CMS) played a pivotal role during this period, running both a mission and the Nugent Memorial Intermediate School in the area1. Mission

St.Daniel University is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, character development, and community engagement. We strive to empower our students with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and a sense of social responsibility. Through innovative teaching methods and a nurturing environment, we prepare our students to become lifelong learners and active contributors to society.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution in South Sudan, recognized for its commitment to quality education, ethical values, and holistic growth. We envision graduates who are compassionate, globally aware, and equipped to address the challenges of the 21st century. St.Daniel University aims to create a positive impact on the lives of our students, their families, and the broader community.
